Bolognese

Bolognese is a delicious Italian meat sauce

This is filler text describing the sauce. It is delicious. The sauce contains sauteed veggies, pork, beef.

This is more filler text. I prefer to serve the sauce over rigatoni. Other common pasta options include fettucine and spaghetti. The sauce it so delicious that I'd have no problem eating it without pasta, as a sort of chili.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Beef
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine
  • Milk
  • Beef Broth

Steps

  1. Brown beef & pork mixture, then remove from pan
  2. Sautee chopped vegetables in olive oil and butter
  3. Stir in tomato paste
  4. Add meat back to pan
  5. Add red wine
  6. Simmer for 3-6 hours, adding broth if it gets too dry
  7. 30 minutes before serving, add milk and stir to combine
