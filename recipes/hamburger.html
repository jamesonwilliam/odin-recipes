Cheeseburger

A cheeseburger is a classic American sandwich

This is filler text describing the burger. Burgers are the best. I like mine with onions, cheese, ketchup, and mayo.

This is more filler text. I prefer my burgers cooked medium well, with a little bit of pink. I like my steaks medium rare, but for some reason I like my burgers cooked a little bit more.

Ingredients

  • Burger patty
  • Onions
  • American cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Cook burger on both sides
  2. When almost done cooking, add cheese to burger
  3. Chop onions
  4. Place burger on bun
  5. Add condiments
  6. Enjoy!
