Mac and Cheese

Mac and cheese is an easy meal in a pinch

This is filler text describing the dish. I love mac and cheese. I prefer Annies brand of mac and cheese. Some people prefer Kraft.

This is more filler text. This is an extremely easy dish to make. If you can boil water, you can make mac and cheese

Ingredients

  • Box of mac and cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add pasta
  3. Cook for 8-10 minutes
  4. Drain pasta, then add back to pan
  5. Stir in milk, butter, and packet of cheese powder
  6. Enjoy!
